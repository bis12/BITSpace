BITSpace | AI based game

Welcome to BITSpace, a canvas based game with educational AI based play. The objective of the game is for your fleet to be the last remaining fleet at the end of a battle.

You build a fleet over time by designing ships with different sensors, weapons, and armor. You can upgrade ships and equipment with points you earn by participating in battles.

The interesting part is that you do not interact with the battles in any way. You create your own AI to control each ship based on input from the sensors you've attached. In addition, you create a commander AI that sets a general strategy for your force. Click below to try it out.
